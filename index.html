Property Description

Tucked in the vibrant heart of Siesta Key Village, this beautifully updated villa offers the perfect blend of coastal living and modern convenience. Rarely available, this 3-bedroom, 2-bath home is part of the highly desirable Whispering Sands community, an active 55+ enclave with its own private beach just a short five-minute stroll from your front door. This beach is truly one of a kind, featuring rare ancient quartz sand that is 99% pure and originally formed in the Appalachian Mountains.

Inside, the home has been tastefully remodeled with tile flooring throughout, a refreshed kitchen featuring all-new lower cabinets with stone countertops, complemented by newly painted upper cabinets in the same high-quality construction and style for a seamless look. Stainless steel appliances complete the modern kitchen design. Both bathrooms have been completely renovated for a clean, contemporary feel.

The spacious primary suite includes a large walk-in closet with a makeup vanity and its own private patio. A second patio off the main living area creates an inviting space for outdoor lounging or dining. Designed for comfort and independence, this villa shares no common walls and is fully air conditioned, including the garage, which also features its own dedicated AC unit.

Recent improvements provide peace of mind, with hurricane-rated impact windows and sliders installed in 2022, a new roof, updated plumbing, and durable plastered concrete masonry walls. Whispering Sands offers an exceptional lifestyle with two sparkling pools, one just steps away and another at the private beachfront, plus community security and an active social calendar.
